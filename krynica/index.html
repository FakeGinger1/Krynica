Why Krynica-Zdrój?



What Krynica-Zdrój Offers


If you're seeking for a vivid place to spend a serene weekend or few weeks during any time of the year, then Krynica-Zdrój is a more niche destination which will provide you with a beautiful glimpse into Polands rich history.

Tucked in between the Beskids Sadeczki mountains of southern Poland, this charming town offers:

  • Great winter sport facilities
  • Hiking and biking trails as well as picturesque walking paths
  • Mineral water houses
  • A tree-top path and 49.5 metre tower
  • Many historic museums.
