Recommended Spots!

Góra Parkowa

Krynica-Zdrój sits right at the base of the Parkowa mountain (Góra Parkowa) which reaches an altitude of 741 meters. By ascending the mountain via a series of wooden sloped pathways to view the following:

You can also get to the peak by riding the funicular train (built in 1937) to more quickly see the tourist attractions at the top. During the winter it is used for skiing, snowboarding and sledding, with beautiful immersive light displays at night. In the summer exhibitions are put up showing the history of the town and you can better enjoy the surrounding Zdrojowy park

Tree-top Tower

Walk this 1,030 metre pathway among the tall pine tree forest of Slotwiny. This wooden lookout tower reaches 49.5 meters high which equals to 15 stories tall! The wooden path is decorated by information boards providing context about the surrounding wildlife, nature and history. Finish this spectacular walk off with a quick descent on a 60 metre slide, not just for kids! During the summer concerts are held below the tower, pairing music with an exceptional view. In the winter take the short walk to the neighbouring ski resort to make the most of the winter snowfall.
